A Brief Timeline

Big Moments

  • First Message

    March 1, 2017

    On February 25, 2017, Lucas changes his password on a few dozen websites in response to Cloudbleed. As luck would have it, one of these is OKCupid.

    A few days later, Katrina sees Lucas's profile and decides to make the first move.

  • First Date

    March 16, 2017

    Katrina & Lucas meet up for ice cream at CREAM on 16th & Valencia in San Francisco.

    Playing it safe, Lucas orders his ice cream in a cup. Katrina goes for it and gets the messy ice cream sandwich, which she inevitably spills on her dress.

  • In a Relationship

    April 27, 2017

    Katrina & Lucas decide they're ready to make it official after their eighth date at the Potrero Stage.

    They take their first photograph together at Inspiration Point three days later.

  • "I Love You"

    August 12, 2017

    Love is in the air under the starry night sky at this family wedding in Southern Ontario.

    Almost in unison, Katrina & Lucas both share these words with each other for the first time (okay, Katrina was technically first).

  • First Place Together

    September 1, 2018

    Katrina & Lucas find an apartment together in the Haight-Ashbury district.

    The search was swift, having visited the apartment on their first day of tours.

  • Introducing Morgana

    October 20, 2019

    Katrina & Lucas adopt a tuxedo cat from Give Me Shelter Cat Rescue.

    She is given the name Morgana, after the character from Persona 5 as well as Morgana from Arthurian legend.

  • Engagement

    November 18, 2021

    Lucas pops the question at Magic Kingdom in Central Florida. Katrina says "Yes"!

    Katrina Disneybounds as the dining scene from Lady and the Tramp, only it is a bella giornata instead of a bella notte.

  • Wedding

    February 19, 2023

    Katrina & Lucas plan to tie the knot at the Temescal Beach House in Oakland.

    They look forward to celebrating with their closest friends and family.
