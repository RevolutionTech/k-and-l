House Rules

Keep in Mind

Our wedding venue is located within the East Bay parks, and protection of the natural area is a priority to the community. There are a few rules about the venue that we'd like to notify guests of. Following these will help us to conserve the area, and also help us get back our venue deposit. Thank you for your support!

  • No smoking of any kind is allowed in the East Bay parks. This includes e-cigarettes and vape pens as well. Because the venue is within the East Bay park, you'll have to walk outside of the area to smoke (see map above).
  • No hard liquor is allowed on-site. That's what the after-party is for. 😁 The bar will be stocked with beer & wine.
  • Non-service animals aren't allowed inside of the building.
  • Please leave your confetti, glitter, rice, birdseed, petals, streamers, and poppers at home! They're bad for the birds. Fireworks and sparkles are also not allowed.
  • Depending on how things go, some COVID-19 restrictions may be in place (for example: testing, proof of vaccination, or face covering requirements). We will update everyone accordingly as we know more, closer to the event.
